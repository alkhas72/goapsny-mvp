## Product invariants

- Map is visible without registration. Authentication may be absent and data still loads.
- Gray means “предварительно, ожидает проверки сообщества”; it is visible immediately and must never be presented as coordinator-approved.
- Map is the background; menu/filter/card are overlays, not separate mini-site pages.
- Legend appears only in Window 1 and never repeats as a permanent map legend.
- Every status is word + color. Red remains visible. Portable ramp is purple `#7A5AF8` plus a text label.
- Top rail after welcome: logo/name, `ab · ru · en`, menu. Search/filter is the second line on the map.
- Bottom rail is constant: Instagram, Telegram, Cabinet centered, site. Omit the unresolved email door for this G1 candidate.
- Temporary G1 candidate for the unresolved card fork: keep the bottom rail visible under the sheet. Record this as reversible; Alkhas decides at G1.
- Use real SVG/Lucide icons, never emoji UI icons.
- No animation pass. If any transition is necessary, keep it under 300 ms and honor `prefers-reduced-motion`.
- System font is the explicit sprint fallback because `DESIGN.md` has not finalized the local Abkhaz font. Do not fetch a font from a CDN or choose a permanent font in T1.
